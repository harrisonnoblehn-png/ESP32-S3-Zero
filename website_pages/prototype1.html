Overview

Prototype #1 lacked any strangth to hold its support beams and broke. needs complete change in design for a more durable design

Prototype #1

Printing information

Notes

This design original had four beams in the corners which were supposed to support a locking aspect but I overestimated the size of the print. The design was way too small to produce. Next design needs better ventilation.

Review

Design lacked strength to hold, corner support beams broke fast and latch attempt was not working. We will need to make the build more simple and have flat blocky features. This design allowed me to realise the limits of the printer and to focus on simple shapes over complex shapes.
